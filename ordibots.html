WHAT IS ORDIBOTS 3D

Ordibots 3D is the third experiment in a series of experiments in the ordinal ecosystem that seeks to deal with the problems of network fees and block usage that arise during the process of creating generative on-chain art on the bitcoin blockchain
The first experiment saw the development of the Generative-BRC-721 and the creation of the first GBRC-721 ordinals collection called Ordibots through a process called deployment and minting in which you establish the art's base and traits as the blueprint for your artwork and mint the unique NFOs based on its traits.
This saw the cost of inscription of a whole collection drop by 98%, the deployment of the BRC69-standard which was an improvement on the GBRC-721 and the creation of the second experiment in the series called OrdiTroops
Ordibots 3D seeks to be the first to combine the unique style of three dimensional art with the unique experience of Augmented Reality in the Ordinals ecosystemon the bitcoin blockchain
